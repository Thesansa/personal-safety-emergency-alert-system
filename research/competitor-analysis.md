## Comparative Insights

While global applications such as bSafe and Noonlight provide mature emergency monitoring services, they are largely optimized for Western markets with strong emergency dispatch infrastructure. Life360 focuses primarily on family monitoring rather than real-time danger response.

The proposed system differentiates itself by:

Focusing on structured emergency lifecycle management

Introducing hierarchical resolution verification

Targeting South Asian regional constraints

Maintaining a lightweight emergency-first interface
